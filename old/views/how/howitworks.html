Our Idea


Creativity is thinking up new things. Innovation is doing new things.
We encountered a problem

Mocean arises from the discovery of a problem that currently encounters almost everyone involved in the nautical world and it is the fact that boats are prone to motion, which means that all the galley ware can easily fall.

We found an innovative solution

We offer a range of magnetic galley ware products that does not require changing consumer habits or make a specific installation on the boat and can adapt to uneven surfaces and with different inclinations.

Wide range of products

The Mocean range includes everything from tumblers, to wine and champagne flutes, goblets and beer glasses, pitchers, cocktail Bottle Coolers, Wine Decanters and even shot glasses, for stylish entertaining, anywhere.

Hand finished elegance

All Mocean products are hand finished to remove manufacturing join lines, giving the clarity and elegance of glass. Mocean does not shatter making it an extremely safe and cost effective alternative to glass and is dishwasher safe, so will not crack or craze in the dishwasher.

Innovative securing tecnology

All Mocean products are equipped with our special motion secure system. A compatibility system with specially designed place mats for your table and cabinet storage trays.

Easy to use

The storage tray is easy to use, simply touch the tray and your glasses will stick to it due to its unique magnetic properties. If you wish to use them, simply pull them lose of the tray.
